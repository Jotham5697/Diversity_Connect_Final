Welcome to Diversity Connect's Chat Area

This Chat area provides a safe space for students of color to talk about their experiences in their predominantly white institutions (PWI). Here you can talk about disrcimination you faced or saw in your schools, give advice to other students of color, and share anything else you think relates. Feel free to share any experience you feel comfortable enough sharing. If you are sharing someone else's story make sure that you get permission directly from them before sharing. Note: you have the ability to chat anonymously and with that comes a lot of responsibility please make sure to follow the rules located directly below this.

Please add your email in the email section when making your comments. Your email will NOT be displayed or released; it is simply for our records so that we can keep track of our users and offer prizes in the future.

Rules and Regulations

  1. ABSOLUTELY NO RACISM, SEXISM, HOMOPHOBIA OR ANY OTHER FORM OF DISCRIMINATION IS ALLOWED.
      2. The Moderators will delete any message that they deem too offensive or discriminatory.
  2. You are not allowed to use your peers' names in the chat if they are under 18 even with explicit permission.
      3. The use of names for people above the age of 18 is allowed.
  3. Please be sure you proofread your posts before posting because you will be unable to edit them once they are posted.
  4. And most importantly remember that this is a safe space so please feel free to share. Our goal is to build a community where these topics arenâ€™t taboo but rather a conversation that will lead to change.
